Why smoke when you can vape?

Welcome to Monadnock Vapor

For those of you that don't know, Monadnock Vapor is a chain of vapor stores in southern New Hampshire. We proudly serve the towns and surrounding areas of Keene and West Lebanon. Our story is simple. Three guys who used to smoke got into vaping. We decided to open a store. One turned into two. We take our business seriously, and we aim to help people quit smoking and start vaping. We carry high quality products that we actually use. We hired a few very knowledgable and passionate people. We want to help you get everything you need to vape...locally.

We recently just opened up our second location in West Lebanon, NH. Check out the new shop if you're in the area. Grab a bottle of juice, hang out and play some foosball with us.

       
To get the latest news, goings-on and new product info, please check out our social media sites.

What makes us rock?

Fully stocked shop

We carry high quality products that we use.

Are you looking for a starter kit? We got you covered. Or are you into rebuildables on a 100-watt box? Yeah, we can help you out. We carry a full selection of hardware and top shelf juices.
