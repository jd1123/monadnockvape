Why smoke when you can vape?

Welcome to Monadnock Vapor

For those of you that don't know, Monadnock Vapor is a chain of vapor stores in southern New Hampshire. We proudly serve the towns and surrounding areas of Keene and New Bethlehem. Our story is simple. Three guys who used to smoke got into vaping. We decided to open a store. One turned into two. We take our business seriously, and we aim to help people quit smoking and start vaping. We carry high quality products that we actually use. We hired a few very knowledgable and passionate people. We want to help you get everything you need to vape...locally.

Check out our deals and promotions.

What makes us rock?

Fully stocked shop

We carry high quality products that we use.

Are you looking for a starter kit? We got you covered. Or are you into rebuildables on a 100-watt box? Yeah, we can help you out. We carry a full selection of hardware and top shelf juices.

Friendly Staff

Come see our friendly staff.

Maybe you're like us and a serial vapist. Come on in and talk vapor with us. Or maybe you're just getting into vaping and want to learn more. No problem, we will educate you on how to get started and stay off analogs. We're dedicated to our customers. Please, ask around.

Try before you buy

Try all of our juices in the store.

Taste is a personal thing, and who are we to tell you what you like? Try all of our flavors in the store to see if you will like it. We're pretty certain you'll find something that suites you.

Deals and promotions

Here are a few things you should know about our shop. Firstly, you buy 5 house juices, you get the sixth free. That's how it works. No gimmicks. Leave your name at the store, and we'll keep track for you - no punchcards to remember.

Secondly, when you refer a friend who makes a purchase of $15 or more, you get a free house juice. Yep, that's all. It's a thank you for helping us out. We appreciate that.
